$ m new

Show all new mail headers. Alias to 'm headers :n'.

$ m read <mid>

Display message <mid> and mark as read.

$ m compose <recipient>

Compose a new message.

$ m reply <mid>

Reply to message <mid>.

$ m group-reply <mid>

Group-reply to message <mid>. Alias to 'm reply --group <mid>'.

$ m thread <mid>

Show all headers of <mid>'s thread. Alias to 'm headers thread:<mid>'

$ m headers [filter-expression]

Show all headers matching <filter-expression>.
